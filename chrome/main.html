Just Print is a Carleton DevX project. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I choose whether to print double-sided?
Under the hood, Just Print works by sending automated requests to PaperCut's Web Print service. Right now, that service doesn't support double-sided printing- so, neither can we!
Why can't I print more than 10 copies?
Same reason as above.
What do I do if it's just not printing?
Just Print records some errors, but we can't be sure it catches everything. If you notice any bugs (or have feedback of any sort) please let us know at pickartd@carleton.edu.
